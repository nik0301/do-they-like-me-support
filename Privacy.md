# Privacy Policy — Do They Like Me?

Last updated: March 17, 2026

## 1. Overview
Do They Like Me? ("the App") helps users analyze chat conversations and generate reply suggestions.
This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed and how it is used.

## 2. Data You Provide
When you use the App, you may provide:
- Chat transcript text
- Chat screenshots (for text extraction)
- Optional language/locale preferences

## 3. How Data Is Used
Your data is used only to provide core features:
- Extract transcript text from screenshots
- Analyze conversation signals
- Generate suggested replies
- Improve reliability and prevent abuse (limited technical logs/analytics)

## 4. AI and Third-Party Processing
To provide AI features, the App may send required request data to trusted providers, including:
- OpenAI (analysis/reply generation)
- Google Cloud Vision OCR (screenshot text extraction)

Only data necessary for a request is sent.

## 5. Purchases and Billing
Subscriptions/in-app purchases are handled by app stores and RevenueCat.
We do not store full payment card details on our servers.

## 6. Data Retention
- Screenshots are processed in memory by default and are not permanently stored by default.
- We avoid logging raw image bytes and full conversation text in operational logs.
- Limited analytics/technical event data may be retained for product and service operations.

## 7. Security
We use reasonable technical measures (request limits, validation, secure transport, access controls) to protect data.
No system can guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Children
The App is not intended for children under 13 (or higher age where required by local law).

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict personal data.
